COMPANY POLICIES 2026

====================================
1. LEAVE POLICY
====================================

Employees are entitled to 20 days of annual leave per year. 
Sick leave is 10 days per year.
Leave requests must be submitted at least 2 weeks in advance through the HR portal.
Unused leave can be carried forward to the next year up to a maximum of 5 days.

Contact HR for leave approval at hr@company.com

====================================
2. REMOTE WORK POLICY
====================================

Employees may work remotely up to 2 days per week with manager approval.
Remote work equipment will be provided by the company.
Employees must be available during core hours 10 AM - 3 PM.
All remote work must be logged in the time tracking system.

====================================
3. PERFORMANCE REVIEW POLICY
====================================

Performance reviews are conducted bi-annually in January and July.
Employees will receive written feedback and development goals.
Reviews are linked to annual bonuses and promotions.
Self-assessments must be submitted one week before the review meeting.

====================================
4. PROFESSIONAL DEVELOPMENT
====================================

The company provides $2000 annually per employee for professional development.
This can be used for courses, conferences, or certifications.
Approval must be obtained from your manager before enrollment.
Receipts must be submitted for reimbursement within 30 days.

====================================
5. HEALTH AND SAFETY POLICY
====================================

The company is committed to providing a safe work environment.
All incidents must be reported immediately to HR.
Regular safety training is mandatory for all employees.
Emergency exits and fire extinguishers are clearly marked.
First aid kits are available on each floor.

====================================
6. CODE OF CONDUCT
====================================

All employees must maintain professional behavior at work.
Harassment and discrimination of any kind will not be tolerated.
Confidential information must not be shared externally.
Report any violations to HR or use the anonymous hotline.
