Remote Work & Hybrid Policy

Effective Date: January 2026

1. Eligibility

Employees who have completed 6 months of employment are eligible for hybrid or remote work.

2. Hybrid Model

Minimum 2 days per week in office.

Core collaboration days: Tuesday and Thursday.

3. Fully Remote Roles

Certain roles approved by leadership may operate fully remote.

4. Working Hours

Standard working hours: 10 AM – 7 PM IST.

Employees must be available during core hours: 11 AM – 4 PM IST.

5. Equipment & Security

Company laptop mandatory.

VPN required for internal system access.

Public WiFi usage without VPN is prohibited.

6. Performance Expectations

Remote employees must maintain productivity metrics.

Managers may revoke remote privileges in case of performance concerns.
