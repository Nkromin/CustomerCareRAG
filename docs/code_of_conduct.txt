Employee Code of Conduct
1. Professional Behavior

All employees must maintain professionalism, integrity, and respect.

2. Harassment Policy

Zero tolerance for harassment, discrimination, or bullying.

3. Data Confidentiality

Sharing confidential data externally without authorization is prohibited.

Violations may lead to termination.

4. Conflict of Interest

Employees must disclose any external employment or financial interests.

5. Disciplinary Action

Violation of company policy may result in:

Warning

Suspension

Termination
