Company Leave Policy

Effective Date: January 2026

1. Annual Leave

All full-time employees are entitled to 24 days of paid annual leave per calendar year.

Leave accrues monthly.

Maximum carry forward allowed: 10 days

Carry forward validity: Must be used within first 6 months of next year.

2. Sick Leave

Employees are entitled to 12 days of paid sick leave annually.

Medical certificate required for leave exceeding 3 consecutive days.

Sick leave cannot be encashed.

3. Casual Leave

Employees receive 8 casual leave days annually.

Cannot be carried forward.

Must be approved at least 2 days in advance (except emergency).

4. Maternity Leave

Female employees are entitled to 26 weeks of paid maternity leave.

Applicable after 80 days of employment.

5. Paternity Leave

Male employees are entitled to 10 working days of paid paternity leave.

6. Leave Approval Process

Employee submits leave request via HR portal.

Reporting manager must approve.

HR final validation required for leave > 5 days.

7. Leave Encashment

Unused annual leave beyond 10 carry-forward days will lapse.
Encashment allowed only during full & final settlement.
