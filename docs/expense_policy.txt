Employee Expense Reimbursement Policy

Effective Date: January 2026

1. Travel Expenses

The company reimburses:

Economy class airfare

AC 2-tier train fare

Cab fare for official travel

Hotel stay up to ₹6000 per night

2. Meal Allowance

₹800 per day for domestic travel

₹2500 per day for international travel

3. Claim Submission

Claims must be submitted within 15 days of expense.

Original bills mandatory.

Claims beyond 30 days require CFO approval.

4. Non-Reimbursable Expenses

Alcohol

Personal shopping

Traffic fines

Luxury hotel upgrades

5. Approval Workflow

Employee submits expense form.

Reporting manager approval.

Finance team verification.

Reimbursement processed within 10 working days.
